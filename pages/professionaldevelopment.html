SASE Resume Database

The SASE Resume Database is a valuable resource for SASE members looking to connect with potential employers. By submitting your resume to our database, you increase your visibility to companies seeking talented individuals in STEM fields. This platform provides a unique opportunity to showcase your skills and experiences to a wide range of industry partners associated with SASE.

To submit your resume or learn more about this opportunity, please visit the official SASE Resume Database page.

Job Opportunities

Handshake

Handshake is Ohio State's job search platform where students can browse positions, schedule interviews, and connect with employers. The platform matches you with opportunities based on your major and interests while providing access to career resources.

Visit Handshake

Career Fairs

Career fairs provide invaluable opportunities to network with potential employers, learn about job openings, and practice your professional communication skills. These events allow you to gain insights into various industries while showcasing your qualifications to hiring managers.

View Career Fairs

SASE Conference

The SASE National Conference is an annual event bringing together all the SASE chapters. It features a career fair, professional development workshops, keynote speakers, and networking opportunities. The conference rotates between major cities each year to maximize accessibility.

Research Opportunities

Undergraduate Research

Engaging in undergraduate research is a transformative experience that can shape your future. By participating in research, you'll develop critical thinking skills, learn advanced methodologies, and gain hands-on experience that sets you apart in graduate school applications and job searches. Working with faculty mentors provides opportunities to contribute to publications, present at conferences, and potentially receive research funding through fellowships and grants.

Explore Research Opportunities

Graduate Research

Discover advanced research opportunities at the graduate level. Whether you're interested in pursuing a master's degree or doctorate, OSU offers extensive research programs across various STEM disciplines. Work with faculty mentors, access state-of-the-art facilities, and contribute to groundbreaking research that shapes the future of science and engineering.

Explore Graduate Research

Volunteer Opportunities
